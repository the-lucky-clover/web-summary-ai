# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** October 2024  
**Effective Date:** October 2024

## Our Privacy Commitment

Web Summarizer AI is built with privacy as the foundational principle. This privacy policy explains our commitment to protecting your privacy and details exactly what data we do (and don't) collect.

## The Simple Truth: We Collect Nothing

**We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data whatsoever.**

## What This Extension Does

This extension:
1. **Extracts text content** from web pages when you explicitly request summarization
2. **Formats prompts** for AI services based on that content
3. **Stores your preferences** locally on your device
4. **Provides an interface** to copy prompts to AI services of your choice

## What This Extension Does NOT Do

❌ **No Data Collection**: We never collect personal information, browsing history, or usage analytics  
❌ **No External Transmission**: Your content never leaves your device through our extension  
❌ **No Tracking**: No cookies, pixels, fingerprinting, or behavioral tracking  
❌ **No Analytics**: No Google Analytics, telemetry, or usage statistics  
❌ **No Storage**: Web content is never stored or cached  
❌ **No Profiling**: No user profiles or behavioral analysis  
❌ **No Advertising**: No ads, affiliate links, or monetization tracking  

## Data Access and Processing

### What We Access
- **Page Content**: Only when you click "summarize" on a specific page
- **YouTube Transcripts**: Only when you request video summarization
- **User Settings**: Stored locally on your device

### How We Process It
- **Locally Only**: All content analysis happens in your browser
- **Temporary**: Content is processed and discarded immediately
- **No Storage**: Content is never saved or cached
- **No Transmission**: Content never leaves your device

### Your Control
- **Explicit Permission**: Content is only accessed when you request it
- **Immediate Processing**: Content is processed and immediately discarded
- **Local Settings**: All preferences stored locally, never synchronized

## Local Data Storage

The extension stores these items **locally on your device only**:

- ✅ Summary length preference (short/medium/detailed)
- ✅ Language preference
- ✅ Interface settings (floating button, context menu)
- ✅ Installation date and version number

**Important**: This data is stored using Chrome's local storage API and never leaves your device.

## Permissions Explained

### `activeTab`
- **Purpose**: Access current page content when you use the extension
- **Scope**: Only the tab you're actively using
- **When**: Only when you click the summarize button
- **Data**: Temporary access to page text for prompt generation

### `storage`
- **Purpose**: Save your preferences locally
- **Scope**: Local device storage only
- **Data**: Your extension settings and preferences
- **Sync**: Never synchronized with external services

### YouTube Host Permission
- **Purpose**: Extract video transcripts when available
- **Scope**: Only YouTube video pages you choose to summarize
- **When**: Only when you request video summarization
- **Data**: Video transcript text (if available)

## Third-Party Services

### What We Don't Do
- **No API Calls**: We don't make calls to AI services on your behalf
- **No Data Sharing**: We don't send your content to any external service
- **No Integrations**: No hidden integrations with analytics or tracking services

### What You Do
When you copy our generated prompts to AI services:
- **Your Choice**: You decide which AI service to use
- **Your Control**: You paste the content yourself
- **Their Policies**: AI service privacy policies apply to that interaction
- **No Tracking**: We don't track which services you use
